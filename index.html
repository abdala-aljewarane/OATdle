How to Play

Welcome to OATdle! Here's how to play:

  • A new OAT practice question is available each day.
  • Read the question and select one of the four multiple-choice answers (A, B, C, or D).
  • You can click the option with your mouse or use your keyboard (A, B, C, D keys).
  • You only get one attempt per question!
  • After you answer, the correct answer will be highlighted in green, and an incorrect selection will be shown in red.
  • An explanation for the correct answer will appear below the question.
  • Your statistics are tracked automatically. Click the üìä icon to see your progress.
  • A new question will be available at midnight.
